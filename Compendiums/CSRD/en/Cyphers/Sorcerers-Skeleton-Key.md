## Sorcerer's Skeleton Key    
>[!info] Stats    
> **Level:** 1d6+2    
> **Form:** Wooden Stick, Iron Wand, or Piece of Straw  
    
When tapped three times against any locked door or other object (of the cypher level or lower), the key automatically unlocks it.
