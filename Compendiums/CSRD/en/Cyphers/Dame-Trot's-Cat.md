## Dame Trot's Cat  
>Level: 1d6+3  
  
>[!info] Form  
>Statue of a Cat
  
When activated by feeding it a bit of milk or fish, the statue protects the user, yowling and hissing the next time it senses danger. The cat's level is equal to the cypher level.
