## Friction Reducer    
>[!info] Stats    
> **Level:** 1d6    
> **Kind:** Manifest  
    
Spread across an area up to 10 feet (3 m) square, this makes things extremely slippery. For one hour per cypher level, movement tasks in the area are hindered by three steps.
