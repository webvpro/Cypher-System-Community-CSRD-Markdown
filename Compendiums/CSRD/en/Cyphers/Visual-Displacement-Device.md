## Visual Displacement Device    
>[!info] Stats    
> **Level:** 1d6    
> **Kind:** Fantastic  
    
Projects holographic images of the user to confuse attackers. The images appear around the user, giving them an asset to Speed defense actions for ten minutes per cypher level.
