## Flaming Arrow  
>[!info] Stats  
> **Level:** 1d6+2  
> **Form:** Arrow with a Silver-White Shaft, Golden Head, and Fletching of Peacock Feathers  
> **Kind:** Fairy Tale
  
The arrow explodes into flame when it strikes something, inflicting its level in damage to all within immediate range.
