## Warmth  
>[!info] Stats  
> **Level:** 1d6  
> **Kind:** Manifest
  
Keeps the user warm and comfortable, even in the harshest cold temperatures, for twenty-four hours. During this time, the user has Armor equal to the cypher's level that protects against cold damage.
