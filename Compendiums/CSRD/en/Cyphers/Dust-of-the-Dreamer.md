## Dust of the Dreamer  
>Level: 1d6  
  
>[!info] Form  
>Pouch of Very Fine, Rainbow-Hued Dust
  
When sprinkled in the eyes, grants the recipient all the benefits of a ten-hour recovery roll as a single action. This does not use up any of their recovery rolls.
