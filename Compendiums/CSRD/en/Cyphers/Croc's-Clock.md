## Croc's Clock  
>Level: 1d6  
  
>[!info] Form  
>Tiny Ticking Clock, No Bigger than a Thumbnail
  
When attached to (or swallowed by) a living creature or an object, the clock ticks loudly, alerting everyone within long range to its presence for one day.
