## Teleporter Bounder    
>[!info] Stats    
> **Level:** 1d6+2    
> **Kind:** Fantastic  
    
User teleports up to 100 x the cypher level in feet to a location they can see. They arrive safely with their possessions but cannot take anything else with them.
