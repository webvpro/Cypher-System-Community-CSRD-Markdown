## Force Shield Projector    
>[!info] Stats    
> **Level:** 1d6+3    
> **Kind:** Fantastic  
    
Creates a shimmering energy shield around the user for one hour, during which time they gain +3 to Armor (or +4 to Armor if the cypher is level 5 or higher).
