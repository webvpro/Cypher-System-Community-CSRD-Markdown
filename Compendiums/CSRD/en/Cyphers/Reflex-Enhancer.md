## Reflex Enhancer    
>[!info] Stats    
> **Level:** 1d6    
> **Kind:** Subtle  
    
All tasks involving manual dexterity-such as pickpocketing, lockpicking, juggling, operating on a patient, defusing a bomb, and so on-are eased by two steps for one hour.
