## Poison (Mind Disrupting)  
>[!info] Stats  
> **Level:** 1d6+2  
> **Kind:** Manifest
  
The victim suffers Intellect damage equal to the cypher's level and cannot take actions for a number of rounds equal to the cypher's level.
