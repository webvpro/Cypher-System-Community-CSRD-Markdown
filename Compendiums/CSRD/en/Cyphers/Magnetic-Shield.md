## Magnetic Shield  
>[!info] Stats  
> **Level:** 1d6+2  
> **Kind:** Fantastic
  
For ten minutes per cypher level, metal objects cannot come within immediate range of the user. Metal items already in the area when the device is activated are slowly pushed out.
