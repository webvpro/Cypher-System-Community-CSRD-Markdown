## Force Screen Projector    
>[!info] Stats    
> **Level:** 1d6+3    
> **Kind:** Fantastic  
    
Creates an immobile plane of solid force up to 20 feet by 20 feet (6 m by 6 m) for one hour. The plane conforms to the space available.
