## Vase of Tears  
>[!info] Stats  
> **Level:** 1d6  
> **Form:** Vase, Vial, or Jar Filled with Tears  
> **Kind:** Fairy Tale
  
Breaking the vase creates a protective spell around the character, preventing them from taking any Might damage the next time they are physically attacked.
