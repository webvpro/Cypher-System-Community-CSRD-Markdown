## Jack's Candlestick  
>[!info] Stats  
> **Level:** 1d6+3  
> **Form:** Burning Candlestick  
> **Kind:** Fairy Tale
  
Jumping over the candlestick restores a number of points equal to the cypher's level to the user's Speed Pool.
