## Ray Emitter Fear    
>[!info] Stats    
> **Level:** 1d6+2    
> **Kind:** Fantastic  
    
Allows the user to project a ray up to long range (very long range if the cypher is level 6 or higher) that causes the target to flee in terror for one minute.
