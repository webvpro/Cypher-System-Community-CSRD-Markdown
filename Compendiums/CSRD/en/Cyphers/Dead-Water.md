## Dead Water    
>[!info] Stats    
> **Level:** 1d6+3    
> **Form:** Vial, Pot, or Jar of Black Liquid    
> **Kind:** Fairy Tale  
    
Brings a character back to life. However, they come back with a permanent 3-point reduction in their maximum Might Pool.
