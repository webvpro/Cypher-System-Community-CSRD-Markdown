## Efficacy Boost    
>[!info] Stats    
> **Level:** 1d6+1    
> **Kind:** Power Boost  
    
This cypher boosts an ability that requires a skill roll. The use of the ability is eased (eased by two steps if the cypher is level 5 or higher).
