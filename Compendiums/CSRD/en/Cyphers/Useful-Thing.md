## Useful Thing  
>[!info] Stats  
> **Level:** 1d6+1  
> **Kind:** Scavenger Subtle
  
One item from the Useful Stuff table is gained; roll randomly. If the cypher's level is 6 or higher, the character can choose which item they obtain from the table.
