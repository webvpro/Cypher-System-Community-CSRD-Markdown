## Bowl of Porridge    
>[!info] Stats    
> **Level:** 1d6+3    
> **Form:** Just-Right Bowl of Porridge    
> **Kind:** Fairy Tale  
    
Restores a number of points equal to the cypher level to the user's Might Pool. Also protects the user from the effects of cold for ten minutes.
