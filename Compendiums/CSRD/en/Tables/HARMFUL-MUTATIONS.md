D100 Harmful Mutations

| 31–40              | Malformed brain                                                                                              | All memory‑ or cognitive‑related tasks are hindered.                                                                                                         |
| 41–45              | Mentally vulnerable                                                                                          | All Intellect defense tasks are hindered                                                                                                                     |
