## Masters the Swarm  
Insects. Rats. Bats. Even birds. You master one type of small creature that obeys you.  

>[!info] Intrusions  
>- A command is misunderstood.  
>- Control is erratic or is lost.  
>- Bites and stings are not uncommon for masters of the swarm.
