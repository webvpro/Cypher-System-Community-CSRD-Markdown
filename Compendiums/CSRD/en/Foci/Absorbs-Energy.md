## Absorbs Energy    
You can harness kinetic energy and transform it into other kinds of energy.    
  
>[!info] Intrusions    
>- Energy goes to ground in a destructive way.    
>- Some predators feed directly on energy.    
>- An unintended item is drained of energy.
