## Explorer's Gloves  
>[!info] Stats  
> **Level:** 1d6  
> **Form:** Thick but Flexible-Fingered Leather Gloves  
> **Depletion:** Thick but Flexible-Fingered Leather Gloves  
> **Kind:** Fantasy
